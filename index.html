Workout Plan

How to Use

Do 3 rounds. Each movement is 40 seconds with 20 seconds of rest. Keep the pace controlled. The goal is to wake up, build strength, and avoid joint irritation before work.

Over-40 Form Rules
Move slow. Stop sharp pain. Use a chair, wall, counter, or shorter range of motion when needed. Finish feeling worked, not wrecked.
